Few lesson from project

April 11, 2017

I have worked on testing projects and my team handles developing the test app. I thought, writing the app is difficult without reference. We don't have many references but few specifications.

After a while, my colleague Avan took up this task. We had experience testing similar types of activity.

Avan and I usually go out for lunch. That was the third day he started developing the app. As a coincidence, we met Avan's friend during our lunch time. We talked about his friend, office, how his company is performing and other things too.

His friend joked and said, Avan was working late at night at home. I didn't guess, he worked for test app development. On the same evening, he shared the finished test app code.

I realized later, because of his commitment and enthusiasm, he completed the task. As well as he referred to some old code & docs for this.

Instead of thinking, it's difficult to develop. If I believed and started the task, this could have been done. This is how I learned to do my task:

  • Believe and start yourself (you can do).
  • Consider all the resources available in hand or with us.
